## Reproducibility FAQ

### Q1: As an author, am I required to full out the reproducibility checklist in the full-paper submission form? 

A1: Yes. You won't be able to submit the full paper without filling out the reproducibility checklist first. Note that some items in the checklist may not be relevant to your submission. For example, a dataset paper might have no code to be released. In that case, you choose "Not applicable"
